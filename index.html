Caroline Sigl

Follow these steps to view the source code of any website:

  1. Open up your website of choice in Google Chrome.
  2. Right click anywhere on the page.
  3. Choose the inspect option from the dropdown menu.
  4. Toggle to the Sources pane on the top of the page.

DevTools can also help you do these other cool things:

  • You can see how your website would look with a different arrangement of elements by clicking and dragging the three dots next to a text element or div in the Elements pane.
  • DevTools can help trace down syntax errors that cause your webiste to break. Check the Console pane to debug.
  • You can see how your divs would look with different colors or styles by temporarily modifying the element.style code when you hover over a div in the elements pane.


"Using DevTools is like...looking at the recipe card after having a delicious meal. You can see the components of the website seperated like the ingredients list in a recipe. You can also see the directions or javascript code for how it was baked together. If you have ideas for how to improves the dish, you can make notes right on the recipe card." -Me
